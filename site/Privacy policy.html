Privacy Policy

At Voyage TRVL, we value the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we may collect when you use our app, how we use and safeguard that information, and your rights in relation to your personal data.

Information We Collect

When you use the Voyage TRVL app, we may collect certain personal information from you, including:

  • Personal identification information: such as your name, email address, phone number, and billing information when you make a booking or create an account.
  • Travel preferences: such as your destination preferences, travel dates, and accommodation preferences, to provide personalized travel recommendations.
  • Location information: if you grant us permission, we may collect and process information about your precise location to provide location-based services and recommendations.
  • Usage data: we may collect information about how you interact with the app, such as your search queries, pages viewed, and features used, to improve our app and provide a better user experience.
  • Communication data: including any communication you send to us, such as inquiries, feedback, or support requests.

How We Use Your Information

We may use the personal information we collect from you for the following purposes:

  • Providing services: to fulfill your travel bookings, communicate with you regarding your bookings, and provide customer support.
  • Personalization: to personalize your experience, provide tailored travel recommendations, and show relevant offers and advertisements.
  • Analytics and improvements: to analyze user behavior, improve our app, and enhance our services.
  • Communications: to send you promotional emails, newsletters, or other marketing materials. You can opt out of these communications at any time by following the instructions provided in the emails.
  • Legal obligations: to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and employ industry-standard security measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please understand that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

We may engage third-party companies or individuals to provide services on our behalf, such as payment processing, data analysis, customer support, or marketing assistance. These third parties may have access to your personal information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to:

  • Access and obtain a copy of your personal information.
  • Rectify any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Request the erasure of your personal information.
  • Object to the processing of your personal information.
  • Restrict the processing of your personal information.
  • Data portability, or request the transfer of your personal information to another organization.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights or have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us using the contact information provided on our website.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes we make will be posted on this page, and we will notify you by email or through a notice in the app prior to the changes taking effect.
